Manchester Airport at Ringway opens

25/06/1938

Manchester Airport (IATA: MAN, ICAO: EGCC) is an international airport at Ringway, Greater Manchester, England, 7.5 nautical miles (13.9 km; 8.6 mi) south-west of Manchester city centre. In 2016, it was the third busiest airport in the United Kingdom in terms of passenger numbers and the busiest of those not serving London.
